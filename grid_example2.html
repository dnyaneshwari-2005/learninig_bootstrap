About The car

The Toyota Fortuner and Mahindra Thar are both popular SUVs in India, but they cater to different needs and preferences. The Fortuner is a larger, more luxurious SUV known for its reliability and powerful engine options, while the Thar is a more rugged, off-road focused vehicle with a focus on durability and adventurous spirit.
